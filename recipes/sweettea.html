Steak Fries

Description

Nectar of the gods. Referred to as "tea" in the American South.

Ingredients

  • 3 family-sized black tea bags.
  • 1 pinch baking soda
  • 2 cups sugar
  • lemon slices (optional)

Steps

  1. Fill a 3 quart saucepan 3/4 full of water and add tea bags with strings tied together. Bring to a boil over medium-high heat
  2. Once boiling, remove saucepan from heat. When the water stops bubbling, flick a pinch of baking soda into the saucepan.
  3. Put 2 cups sugar into a gallon pitcher. While holding the tied tea bag strings, pour liquid contents of the saucepan into the pitcher, then stir until sugar is no longer stuck to the bottom of the pitcher.
  4. Refill the saucepan with water and dunk tea bags for one minute, then pour liquid contents into the pitcher while holding the tied tea bag strings and stir pitcher contents again. Repeat this step until the gallon pitcher is full and well-stirred.
  5. Refrigerate until cold and serve over ice. Garnish with lemon slices if preferred.
