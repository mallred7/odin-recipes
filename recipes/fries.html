Steak Fries

Description

Chunky, seasoned fries, bakes easily in the oven.

Ingredients

  • 4 russet potatoes
  • 1 Tbsp cooking oil
  • 1/2 Tbsp chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425ÂºF.
  2. While the oven is preheating, wash potatoes and cut lengthwise into wedges.
  3. Put the potato wedges in a bowl with cooking oil and spices, tossing until coated.
  4. Place wedges on a baking sheet lined with aluminum foil and bake in the oven for 35 minutes or until browned.
