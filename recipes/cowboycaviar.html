Cowboy Caviar

Description

A dippable bean salad with TexMex flavors.

Ingredients

  • 1 can black beans
  • 1 can black eyed peas
  • 1 bell pepper
  • 1 red onion
  • 2 roma tomatoes
  • 1/4 bunch cilantro
  • 2 Tbsp olive oil
  • 2 Tbsp lime juice
  • 1/2 Tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp ground cumin

Steps

  1. Drain and rinse the black beans and black eyed peas in a colander before adding to a large bowl.
  2. Finely dice the bell pepper, onion, tomatoes, and cilantro, then add to the bowl with the beans.
  3. Whisk together the remaining ingredients in a small bowl, then pour into the large bowl and stir until all ingredients are well mixed.
